User Story

As a developer
i want to learn self awareness
so that i can work on a team in the best way i can.

Commentary

I am a type 9: The Peacemaker

Strengths:

Type nines are usually calm and level headed. They can also difuse arguments easily by taking into account different views.

Weaknesses:

Type nines tend to not speak out very often so their voice is not always heard

So, in a team type nines may not always speak their mind. But when they do, they have well thought-out explanations and thoughts. They can also help the team work better together by disputing conflicts.
